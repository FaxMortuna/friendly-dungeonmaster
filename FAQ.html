How does the name generator work?
The generators randomly combine selected names, flavors or other stuff I have gathered, to create a unique inspiration.
Where do you get your ideas from?
I mainly use my own ideas, but I also draw inspiration from various sources such as books, movies, and games.
Can I submit my own suggestions?
Yes! You can submit suggestions and feedback through the contact form. I appreciate every idea.
Will my data be stored?
No, no personal data is stored permanently. Suggestions from the contact form are only used for processing.
Is this for free?
Yes, this service is completely free to use. I started it to learn a bit about web development in the first place.
